Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of their data from our Auto-Poster application.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Delete via Facebook (Recommended)

  1. Log in to your Facebook account
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings
  3. Click on Apps and Websites in the left menu
  4. Find "Auto-Poster" in your list of apps
  5. Click Remove next to the app name
  6. Confirm the removal when prompted

This will immediately revoke the app's access to your Facebook data.

Option 2: Email Deletion Request

If you want to ensure all backup data is removed, you can email us directly:

  • Email: danasbihac@gmail.com
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request - Auto-Poster
  • Include: Your Facebook user ID (if known) and the email associated with your account

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation code.

Option 3: Facebook Data Deletion Callback

If you're a developer or have technical knowledge, you can also trigger the data deletion callback through your Facebook settings. When you remove the app, Facebook automatically sends a deletion request to our system.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete:

  • Any stored access tokens for your Facebook account
  • Your Facebook user ID and associated information
  • Any logs or records containing your personal information

Confirmation Process

After we process your deletion request:

  1. You will receive a confirmation email with a unique confirmation code
  2. You can use this code to verify that your data has been deleted
  3. If you requested deletion via email, we'll confirm once the process is complete

Contact Information

For any questions about data deletion:
Email: danasbihac@gmail.com

This app is for personal use only. We take your privacy seriously and will promptly honor all deletion requests.
