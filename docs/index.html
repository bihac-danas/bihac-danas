Privacy Policy for Auto-Poster App

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our auto-poster application ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

When you use our app to connect to Facebook, we may collect:

  • Facebook Page Information: We access your Facebook Page to post content on your behalf. This includes your Page name, Page ID, and the ability to create posts.
  • Access Tokens: We store authentication tokens that allow the app to post to your Facebook Page. These tokens are stored securely and used only for their intended purpose.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To post content to your Facebook Page as you instruct
  • To maintain and improve the app's functionality
  • To troubleshoot technical issues

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Facebook, as necessary to provide the service (as governed by Facebook's terms)
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. Access tokens are stored securely and are only used for the specific Pages you authorize.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect the app from your Facebook Page at any time
  • Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion section below)
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from our app:

  1. Remove the app from your Facebook account by going to Settings > Apps and Websites in your Facebook profile
  2. Contact us at danasbihac@gmail.com with your Facebook user ID to request complete data deletion
  3. We will confirm deletion within 30 days

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
